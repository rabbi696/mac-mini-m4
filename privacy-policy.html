Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

At Mac Software Support, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our website and services.

- To send periodic emails regarding updates, promotions, or changes to our services.

- We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

4. Sharing Your Information

- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights

- You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.

- Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect the functionality of the site.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mac@golamrabbi.dev.
